Tracks

Hack Track

The Hack track is geared towards students who have an interest in computer science and those who want to build a working prototype. In other words, this is your typical hackathon competition.

Criteria:
Originality/Creativity (33%)
Practical Implementation (33%)
Technical Complexity (33%)

Design Track

The Design track is for students who do not enjoy programming but have a creative passion for making things! For this track, you need to submit a mockup and design of your idea.

Criteria:
Originality/Creativity (33%)
Practical Implementation (33%)
Completeness and Polishness (33%)
